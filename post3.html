5 Smart Home Devices That Make Life Easier & Safer

By Aging Gracefully at Home | August 14, 2025

Smart home technology isn't just for tech enthusiasts; it's a powerful tool for creating a more accessible, convenient, and secure home. For those who want to "age in place" with confidence, these devices can offer peace of mind and simplify daily tasks. Here are five smart home devices that are game-changers for comfortable living.

1. Smart Speakers (e.g., Echo Dot or Google Nest)

A smart speaker with a voice assistant is a central hub for your smart home. You can use it to control lights, set reminders for medication, make hands-free calls, or get the weather forecast with just your voice. Its simple interface makes it a great choice for everyone.

2. Smart Plugs and Light Bulbs

Forget fumbling for a light switch or struggling to plug in a lamp. Smart plugs and light bulbs allow you to control any device with your voice or a smartphone app. You can turn lights on before entering a dark room or schedule a coffee maker to start brewing in the morning.

Shop for Smart Bulbs on Amazon

3. Video Doorbells

A video doorbell lets you see and speak to visitors from the safety of your chair or a different room. This is a great security feature that helps you avoid unnecessary trips to the door and screen for packages and deliveries.

Shop for Video Doorbells on Amazon

4. Smart Locks

With a smart lock, you can unlock your door using a key code, a fob, or your smartphone. This eliminates the need for physical keys, which can be hard to handle for those with arthritis. You can also grant temporary access to family members or caregivers when you're not home.

Shop for Smart Locks on Amazon

5. Water Leak Sensors

For added peace of mind, water leak sensors can be placed under sinks, near water heaters, or in other areas prone to leaks. If they detect water, they'll send an alert to your phone, allowing you to address the problem before it causes significant damage. This is especially helpful for preventing costly repairs.
